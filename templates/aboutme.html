My Journey in Ireland

Since I moved to Ireland in 2019, it’s been both exciting and challenging to adapt to a completely new culture and way of life. Every day I discover something new, whether it’s about the people, traditions, or simply learning how things work in a different country.

One of the biggest challenges has been improving my English and integrating into the local community. At the same time, these challenges have pushed me to grow personally and professionally, and I’ve learned to appreciate the beauty of stepping out of my comfort zone.

Living abroad has not only shaped my perspective on life but also motivated me to pursue my passion for technology and web development. This project is part of that journey, where I show my progress, experiences, and lessons learned along the way.
